Ultrawide Video

Note: Force modes disregard fullscreen state and screen size. This feature is designed for watching 4:3 movies on 16:9 displays.

help & about

Ultrawide video is designed to make viewing videos a more pleasurable experience on 21:9 aspect ratio monitors.

You can use the shortcut ctrl+alt+c to toggle between modes while viewing a video.
